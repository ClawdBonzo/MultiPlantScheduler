Privacy Policy

MultiPlant AI Scheduler — Effective March 30, 2026

Overview

MultiPlant AI Scheduler ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your plant care data belongs to you. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores all plant data, care logs, photos, health checks, and watering schedules locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the optional Cloud AI identification feature.

  • Plant names, species, and care settings
  • Watering history and care logs
  • Photos you take within the App
  • Health check records
  • Notification preferences

Cloud AI Plant Identification

When you choose to use the "Get Precise ID" feature, a photo of your plant is sent to a third-party plant identification service (Plant.id by Kindwise) for analysis. This is the only time any data leaves your device. No personal information is attached to these requests — only the plant image is transmitted.

You can review Plant.id's privacy policy at web.plant.id/privacy-policy.

On-Device AI

The App includes an on-device AI model (Apple Core ML) for local plant identification. This runs entirely on your device and sends no data externally.

Subscriptions and Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Subscription management is handled through RevenueCat, which processes anonymous app user IDs. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not include any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage analytics, behavioral data, or device fingerprints.

Notifications

Watering reminders are scheduled locally on your device using iOS notifications. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Data You Share

The App includes optional sharing features (e.g., "Share Your Garden") that let you export a summary of your plant collection. This data is shared only when you explicitly choose to, using the iOS share sheet.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require an account and stores all data locally.

Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time from Settings > Danger Zone > Delete All Data within the App, or by uninstalling the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the "Send Feedback" option in the App's Settings, or email us at multiplantapp@gmail.com.
