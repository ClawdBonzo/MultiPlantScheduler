Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes! All watering reminders, plant tracking, and on-device AI identification work 100% offline. Only the optional "Get Precise ID" cloud feature requires an internet connection.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your Premium subscription or lifetime purchase will be restored automatically through your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > MultiPlant AI Scheduler > Cancel Subscription.

Can I export my plant data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Export All Plants as CSV to download a spreadsheet of your entire plant collection.

How does the AI plant identification work?

The app uses an on-device Core ML model for instant local identification. For higher accuracy, you can use the "Get Precise ID" feature, which sends only the plant photo to a cloud service for analysis.

How do I delete all my data?

Go to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete All Data. You can also uninstall the app to remove all data from your device.
