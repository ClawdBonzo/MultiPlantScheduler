# Privacy Policy for MultiPlant Scheduler

**Last Updated: March 28, 2026**

## Overview

MultiPlant Scheduler ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. The App operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect any personal information. All plant data, schedules, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Apple may collect data related to transactions in accordance with their own privacy policy. The App itself does not receive or store any payment information.

## Camera and Photo Library

The App may request access to your device camera or photo library solely to allow you to add photos of your plants. These images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

## Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you about plant care schedules. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.
